HEARTHLOGS.com A Hearthstone Log Analyzer

Features

  • Win-Loss record keeping
  • Track play mode and ranked mode games separately
  • Win Rating (Did you crush your opponent, or was it a battle to the end?)
  • Track how well your deck is doing over multiple games with insights into how well it plays against other deck archetypes
  • See how well you compare with other people playing similar decks
  • Find errors in your play (i.e. did you have lethal and didn't know it?)
  • And much more...
