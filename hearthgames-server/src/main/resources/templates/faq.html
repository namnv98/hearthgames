Q: Why would I want to Login with Battle.net?
A: HearthGames.com leverages Battle.net APIs to enchance your experience on this website. We use your Battletag to associate games you upload, which allows us to personalize your view of games.
See https://dev.battle.net/docs/read/log_in_with_bnet for more details.
