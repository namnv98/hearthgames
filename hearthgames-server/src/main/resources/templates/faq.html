Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why would I want to Login with Battle.net?
A: HearthGames.com leverages Battle.net APIs to enchance your experience on this website. We use your Battletag to associate games you upload, which allows us to personalize your view of games.
See https://dev.battle.net/docs/read/log_in_with_bnet for more details.
Q: I want to see Arena, Tavern Brawl and Adventure mode games?
A: This is coming "soon". We are focused on Casual and Ranked mode games at the moment.
Q: When I try to login it takes me to the US Battle.net login but I'm not in that region. Will other regions be supported?
A: We plan to support other regions depending on interest. It takes resources to develop a multi-lingual website.
